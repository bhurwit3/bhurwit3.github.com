Burchell, William Travels in the Interior

William Burchell was served an apprenticeship at Kew Royal Gardens before Traveling to South Africa in 1810. He collected over 50,000 specimens and later testified in front of Parliament as an advocate for emigration to the Cape Colony.

Cole, Alfred The Cape and The Kafirs

Alfred Cole was a Colonial Judge who operated on a traveling circuit through rural areas of the Eastern Cape. Cole wrote several books about his experience; this text provides the most detailed descriptions of frontier life, with depictions of English, Dutch, and African characters.
